PURPOSE

Attachments
This procedure shall be completed in a controlled manner to ensure safety of plant personnel, maintain environmental compliance, and prevent equipment damage, in support of the Corpus Christi Complex.
Attachments

The entry point of this procedure is a result of a deviation from normal operating conditions—e.g., when a process variable of concern exceeds normal operating limits.

Attachments

The exit point of this procedure is to initiate the appropriate procedure to return to normal operations. If unable to return to normal operations, initiate the appropriate emergency shutdown procedure (ESD).

RESPONSIBILITY

The Process Manager (PM) and/or  Operator (OP) is responsible for executing this procedure.

Attachments

Any qualified  Process Manager (PM) and/or  Operator (OP) is assigned authority to shut down the process Unit during an emergency without prior approval.

Attachments

Deviation from the procedure must be approved through the deviation process, prior to executing the deviation, in accordance with the Use of Operating Procedures Policy (W-00-P-0012).

Attachments

The Process Manager (PM) or  Operator (OP) will initial at the point that the departure occurs and immediately write an explanation on the reverse side of the page noting the step number, the reason(s) for the departure, and any recommendations regarding the need for procedure revision. The Process Manager (PM) or Operator (OP) will then sign on the reverse page and submit to the Refinery Shift Manager (RSM) for review.
